Shriya,

I’ve been trying to find the right way to say all of this without making it feel too polished, because the truth is I don’t want this to sound polished. I want it to sound like me. And the most honest version is this: I feel incredibly lucky that it’s you.

Different places, different days, same feeling: I missed you deeply, and all that distance really did was make me more sure of how much you matter to me.

In the way where I feel safe with you. In the way where I come back tired from a trip, eat pasta you made, and suddenly feel something in my chest go very still and very sure.

And even though our second date was a complete disaster that should never be mentioned in serious historical records, every date since then has been nothing short of amazing. Which feels right, because that’s what being with you has started to feel like to me: easy, alive, meaningful, and a little unreal in the best way.

Thank you for being someone I can love this openly. Thank you for making room for me in your life the way you have. Thank you for being you.
